1. Who we are

Bonafied Cars Ltd operates bonafied.co.uk — a UK vehicle scoring platform. Contact: admin@bonafied.co.uk. We are the data controller for any personal data processed through this website.

2. What data we collect

Score check requests: when you submit a vehicle registration number, we process it to retrieve and score the vehicle's MOT history. Registration numbers are vehicle identifiers — not personal data — and are not linked to keeper identity.

Email addresses: if you join our mailing list, we store your email in Mailchimp and use it only to send platform launch and update communications. Unsubscribe any time.

Analytics: we collect anonymised usage data via Google Analytics. No personally identifiable information is included.

Score card acknowledgements: when you download a Score Card, we log the timestamp of your acceptance that it represents MOT history data only.

3. How we use your data

To provide the score check service; send launch communications to subscribers; improve the platform through anonymised analytics; and log score card download acknowledgements. We do not sell personal data.

Result pages include clearly labelled affiliate links. We may earn a small commission if you use them. This does not affect your cost or our recommendations. Affiliate relationships exist only where the advice would be the same without the commission.

1. About Bonafied

Bonafied Cars Ltd operates bonafied.co.uk and bonafied.uk. By using this website you agree to these terms.

2. What Bonafied is — and is not

The Bonafied score and tier designation represent an assessment of a vehicle's MOT history only. They are not a comprehensive vehicle history check, mechanical inspection, valuation or guarantee of condition.

Bonafied does not check: declared write-offs (Categories A, B, S or N); outstanding finance; stolen vehicle status; keeper history; or accident damage not recorded at MOT.

An independent HPI check and pre-purchase inspection are strongly recommended before purchasing any used vehicle, regardless of Bonafied score.

3. Score card use by sellers

Sellers downloading a Bonafied Score Card acknowledge that: the score card represents MOT history data only; they are responsible for ensuring their listing is accurate and not misleading; Bonafied accepts no liability for the seller's use of the score card.

Score cards must not be altered, defaced or misrepresented. The disclaimer text must remain visible and unobscured.

4. Limitation of liability

Bonafied Cars Ltd accepts no liability for the condition, fitness for purpose or accuracy of description of any vehicle, or for any loss arising from reliance on the platform's output. The platform is provided on an as-is basis.

5. Mileage and modifications

Mileage consistency is assessed from MOT records only. Odometer fraud cannot be ruled out without independent verification. Bonafied cannot detect vehicle modifications.
